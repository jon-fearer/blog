Welcome to my personal blog! Blogging on various software topics is something that I've wanted to do for a while, but I've always preferred the freedom (and nostalgia) of personal blogs over the large platforms out there. As a result, this is my foray into that world.

My posts on here will mostly be related to programming as well as cloud and data & analytics. I do not plan to add a comments section to this site directly, but if you have any feedback (constructive or otherwise), then feel free to email me at fearer.jon.blog@gmail.com. I may also repost future articles to Hacker News, where there is good comment and voting functionality.

As for the website itself, it was built using Angular 12 and PrimeNG. It is hosted on AWS via a combination of S3 and CloudFront. The source code for this site is openly available on GitHub, so feel free to take a look (or even fork it) for yourself. I plan to add Terraform for the infrastructure piece soon.

Thanks again for visiting! I look forward to improving the site and posting more here in the future.
